About Stackly

The leading force in competitive gaming, tournament organization, and esports intelligence.

Our Purpose

Empowering the Next Generation of Gamers

At Stackly, we believe that esports is more than just gaming—it's a global culture of competition, dedication, and technological advancement. Our mission is to provide the ultimate platform for aspiring players and established professionals alike.

Through our cutting-edge intelligence systems, premium training facilities, and global tournament networks, we are actively shaping the future of digital competition.

Our History

The Evolution of Stackly

2023

Foundation

Stackly was born from a small gaming lounge, organizing local LAN tournaments with a vision for global scale.

2024

First Major Tournament

Hosted the groundbreaking "Neon Series" attracting over 500 teams worldwide, establishing our global presence.

2025

Pro Roster Acquisition

Signed our first tier-1 professional team in Valorant, marking our entry into top-tier competitive play.

2026

Global Hub Launch

Opened our state-of-the-art LA training facility and launched the Stackly ESports Intelligence platform.
